## Full List by Category

### Friendship + Scheduling Mashups
- **FriendSlot** — find the slot to see your friends
- **HangSync** — sync up your hangouts
- **BuddyBlock** — block out time for your buddies
- **FriendPulse** — pulse check on your friendships
- **MeetSpark** — spark the meetup
- **LinkUp Social** — link up your calendars, link up with friends

### Action-Oriented
- **Slotted** — "we're slotted in for Saturday"
- **LockItIn** — "let's lock it in" = confirm plans
- **SlotDrop** — drop a time slot, see who's free
- **PlanDrop** — drop a plan to your friends
- **FreePing** — you're free, send a ping
- **OpenSlot** — find the open slot between friends
- **DownToHang** — "you down to hang?" as an app

### Social Battery Themed
- **FullCharge** — Social Battery is full, let's go
- **GreenLight** — ties to 🟢 Open to plans
- **GoMode** — you're in go mode, let's schedule

### Calendar Wordplay
- **ClearBlock** — your clear blocks, matched with friends
- **TimeMatch** — match your time with theirs
- **FreeFrame** — find the free frame in your week
- **SyncCircle** — sync your circle of friends

### Casual / Young Professional Vibe
- **SquadSync** — sync with your squad
- **HangTime** — it's hang time
- **KickIt** — "let's kick it" = let's hang out
- **PostUp** — "I'm posted up and free"
